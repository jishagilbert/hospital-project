MEDICAL CENTER

Launched in 2002, MEDICAL CENTER is a multi-specialty tertiary care hospital in South India with global outreach. It is the first hospital in India with both National and International accreditations - National Accreditation Board for Hospitals (NABH) and Australian Council on healthcare Standard International (ACHSI). The laboratory of hospital is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration of Laboratories (NABL) and the blood bank accredited by NABH.
